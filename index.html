Challenge

Shifting plans and allocations

As its client pension plans shifted to shared-risk structures and allocations to alternative assets increased (from less than 12% in 2014 to over 32% by 2023), Vestcor required an advanced risk framework to manage increasingly diverse investment pools and tighter risk limits.

The firm needed:

  • A unified risk management platform that supported daily decision-making across front and middle office teams.
  • Tools to align portfolio construction with client-specific objectives.
  • Analytics that could keep pace with its evolving governance and operational models.
